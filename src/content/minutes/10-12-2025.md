## Officer Reports

- EZAA ECOM has been meeting assisting the chapter on endowment efforts and starting planning on the 80th.

### 80th Reunion Planning

- Date has been selected waiting for alumni house to pay deposit to confirm ballroom reservation. From there there are plans to share a save the date and ask for volunteer support on committees.
- Meetings have been occurring regularly with the 80th chairs; Ken Bienstock and Rafael Cenzano on the EZAA side, Steven Pereira and Bea Luers on the Chapter side.
  - Brainstorming for event ideas and things to do over the whole weekend have been discussed.
  - Plans for a variety of service events to allow everyone to participate and have an impact on causes/organizations they connect with.
  - More to come once date and details are confirmed.

### Alumni Donations/Endowment

- Endowment fundraising is going well, the chapter is 32% of the way towards the funds required to start the endowment through RPI.
- Alumni have helped a lot in the fundraising and the chapter with Institute Advancement have plans to continue raising funds and seeking large donations to start the fund.

### National Convention

- Discussion on high costs, $300+ for registration, 120/night for hotel, food, and transportation required.
- Alumni discuss intention to try to support the actives who are planning to attend.
- The chapter has a fundraiser planned to raise funds.
- The chapter will get a confirmed list of actives attending to the EZAA ECOM to be able to plan how to support the actives.
- Talked about what will occur at the National Convention, the discussion was long but can be summarized with details here: https://s2.goeshow.com/apo/bnc/2025/why_attend.cfm
  - Additional information provided on high amounts of legislation and the need for committees, delegates, and alumni volunteers.
  - The chapter and region put forward delegates to vote on the legislative floor.
  - A handful of alumni present already plan to attend.
- APO at Ualbany is hosting an event as a pep rally to prepare for the National convention, EZ brothers plan to attend to prepare.
